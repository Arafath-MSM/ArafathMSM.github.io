Nuwara Eliya District

Tourist Places

  • Pedro Tea Factory: Visit a renowned tea factory and learn about tea production.
  • Hakgala Botanic Gardens: Explore beautiful gardens with a variety of plants and wildlife.
  • Lover's Leap Falls: A picturesque waterfall in the midst of lush greenery.
  • Horton Plains National Park: Discover the unique ecosystem of this national park.
  • Gregory Lake: Enjoy boating and scenic views by the lakeside.
  • Sita Temple: A temple with connections to the epic Ramayana.

Historical Places

  • Nuwara Eliya Post Office: A historic colonial-era building.
  • Adisham Bungalow: A beautiful English-style country house with a rich history.

Camping Spots

  • Horton Plains National Park: Camp in the heart of the national park and witness its unique wildlife.
  • Gregory Lake: Lakeside camping for a serene experience.

5-Star Hotels

  • Heritance Tea Factory: A luxurious hotel in a former tea factory with stunning views.
  • Araliya Green City Hotel: A 5-star hotel in Nuwara Eliya town.

Explore the beauty of Nuwara Eliya District, known for its lush tea gardens, picturesque landscapes, and luxurious accommodations.
