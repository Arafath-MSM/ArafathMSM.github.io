Kandy District

Tourist Places

  • Huluganga Falls: A beautiful waterfall nestled in the scenic hills.
  • Knuckles Mountain Range: A UNESCO World Heritage Site with hiking and natural beauty.
  • World Buddhist Museum: Explore the history of Buddhism.
  • Ranweli Spice Garden: Discover Sri Lanka's rich spice heritage.
  • Maduru Oya National Park: A wildlife sanctuary with diverse flora and fauna.
  • Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya: Stunning gardens with a wide variety of plant species.

Historical Places

  • Temple of the Tooth (Sri Dalada Maligawa): A sacred Buddhist temple housing the relic of the tooth of the Buddha.
  • Gadaladeniya Vihara: A historic temple with unique architecture.
  • Embekka Devalaya: Known for its intricate wood carvings.

Camping Spots

  • Meemure: A remote village offering camping and trekking experiences.
  • Knuckles Conservation Forest: Camping amidst the pristine nature of Knuckles Mountain Range.

5-Star Hotels

  • Earl's Regency Hotel: A luxurious hotel with scenic views and modern amenities.
  • Cinnamon Citadel Kandy: A 5-star hotel overlooking the Mahaweli River.

Experience the natural beauty, history, and culture of Kandy District, Sri Lanka.
