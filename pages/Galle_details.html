Galle District

Tourist Places

  • Galle Fort: Explore the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with its charming streets, ramparts, and Dutch architecture.
  • Galle Lighthouse: Visit the iconic Galle Lighthouse and enjoy panoramic views of the coastline.
  • Galle Fort - Old Gate: Discover the Old Gate, a historic entrance to the Galle Fort.
  • Galle Fort – Main Gate: Explore the Main Gate of Galle Fort, a testament to Dutch colonial architecture.
  • Point Utrecht Bastion: Enjoy the scenic views from Point Utrecht Bastion within Galle Fort.
  • Rumassala Hill: Hike up Rumassala Hill for breathtaking views and visit the Peace Pagoda.
  • Meeran Jumma Mosque: Admire the beauty of Meeran Jumma Mosque, an architectural gem.
  • Dutch Museum: Learn about the colonial history of Galle at the Dutch Museum.
  • National Maritime Museum: Explore the maritime history of Sri Lanka at the National Maritime Museum.

Historical Sites
