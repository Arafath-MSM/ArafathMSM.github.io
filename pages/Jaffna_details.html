Jaffna District

Tourist Places

  • Nallur Kovil: A historic Hindu temple known for its intricate architecture and religious significance.
  • Neduntheevu (Delft Island): An island with unique geological formations and a wild pony population.
  • Nagadipa Purana Vihara: A Buddhist temple with historical importance.
  • Jaffna Market: Explore the vibrant local market offering a variety of goods and local cuisine.
  • Nagadeepa Rajamaha Viharaya: An ancient Buddhist temple with a rich history.

Historical Places

  • Jaffna Fort: A Dutch fort with a tumultuous history.
  • Jaffna Library: Once one of Asia's finest libraries, tragically destroyed but since rebuilt.
  • Manthiri Manai: A historic palace showcasing Jaffna's cultural heritage.

Camping Spots

Camping options may be limited in the urban area of Jaffna. Consider exploring camping opportunities in nearby natural areas.

5-Star Hotels

  • Jetwing Jaffna: A luxury hotel offering modern amenities.
  • The Thinnai: A boutique hotel providing a comfortable stay.
