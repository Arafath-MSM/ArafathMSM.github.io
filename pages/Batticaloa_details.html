Batticaloa District

Tourist Places

  • Batticaloa Fort: Explore the historic Dutch fort with picturesque views.
  • Batticaloa Lagoon: Enjoy a boat ride in the serene lagoon and witness the local fishing culture.
  • Kallady Bridge: Visit the iconic wooden bridge offering stunning sunset views.
  • Kallady Beach: Relax on the sandy shores of this beautiful beach.
  • Kalkudah and Passikudah: Discover pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters.
  • Batticaloa Lighthouse: Climb the lighthouse for panoramic views of the coastline.
  • St Mary's Cathedral: Admire the architecture of this historic cathedral.
  • Unnichchai Tank: Birdwatching and picnicking at this scenic tank.
  • Hindu Temples: Visit local temples and experience the rich culture.
  • Kattankudy Heritage Museum: Explore the heritage and history of the region.

Historical Sites

  • Batticaloa Fort: An iconic fort with a storied history.

Camping Spots

  • Batticaloa Lagoon: Set up camp along the lagoon for a unique experience.

5-Star Hotels

  • Amaya Beach Passikudah: A luxurious beachfront resort offering world-class amenities.
  • Hotel East Lagoon: A 5-star hotel overlooking the scenic lagoon.
