Ampara District

Tourist Places

  • Kumana National Park: Explore the diverse wildlife and bird species in this national park.
  • Gal Oya National Park: Take a safari to witness the natural beauty and wildlife.
  • Deegavapi Stupa: Visit this historic Buddhist stupa with ancient significance.
  • Rajagala: Discover the archaeological site and hike to its summit for panoramic views.
  • Lahugala Magul Maha Viharaya: Explore the ancient Buddhist temple and its surroundings.

Historical Sites

  • Deegavapi Stupa: A historic stupa associated with Buddhist history.

Camping Spots

  • Kumana National Park: Camp in the heart of the national park and connect with nature.
  • Gal Oya National Park - The Crossing: Experience camping in the wilderness.

5-Star Hotels

  • Gal Oya Lodge: A luxury eco-lodge offering a unique and comfortable stay.

Discover the natural beauty, wildlife, and history of Ampara District, where adventure and serenity coexist.
