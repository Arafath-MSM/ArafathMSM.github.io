Kalutara District

Tourist Places

  • Kalutara Bodhiya: Visit the famous Kalutara Bodhiya, an iconic Buddhist stupa.
  • Richmond Castle: Explore the historic Richmond Castle, an elegant mansion with stunning architecture.
  • Kalutara Beach: Enjoy the pristine beaches of Kalutara for sunbathing and relaxation.
  • Thudugala Ella Waterfall: Discover Thudugala Ella Waterfall, a picturesque natural attraction.
  • Fa Hien Cave: Explore the Fa Hien Cave, a historical site with archaeological significance.
