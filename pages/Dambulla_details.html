Dambulla District

Tourist Places

  • Golden Temple of Dambulla: A UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient cave temples.
  • Popham's Arboretum: A lush botanical garden with diverse plant species.
  • Dambulla Dedicated Economic Centre: Experience the local market culture.
  • Ibbankatuwa Megalithic Tombs: Ancient burial sites with historical significance.
  • Dambulla Royal Cave Temple: Explore caves filled with statues and paintings.
  • Sigiriya: A historic rock fortress with stunning frescoes.

Historical Places

  • Archaeological Museum, Dambulla: Learn about the rich history of the region.
  • Aluvihara Rock Cave Temple: A historic cave temple known for its ancient scriptures.

Camping Spots

  • Kandalama Reservoir: Camp by the scenic reservoir surrounded by nature.
  • Minneriya National Park: Enjoy wildlife camping in this national park.

5-Star Hotels

  • Heritance Kandalama: A luxurious hotel with breathtaking views of Sigiriya Rock.
  • Amaya Lake Resort Dambulla: A 5-star resort on the shores of Kandalama Reservoir.
