Colombo District

The Colombo District is known for its capital city, Colombo, which is the largest city in Sri Lanka. Colombo offers a mix of modernity and history, with colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and cultural attractions.

Some of the key places to visit in the Colombo District include:

  • Gangaramaya (Vihara) Buddhist Temple
  • Galle Face Green
  • Viharamahadevi Park
