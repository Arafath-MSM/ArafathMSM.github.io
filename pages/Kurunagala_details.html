Kurunegala District

Tourist Places

  • Silver Temple: Explore the Silver Temple, a renowned religious site in Kurunegala.
  • Munneswaram Temple: Visit the Munneswaram Temple, an ancient Hindu temple of great significance.
  • Yapahuwa: Discover the historic rock fortress of Yapahuwa, an architectural marvel.
  • Panduwasnuwara: Explore Panduwasnuwara, an ancient city with archaeological treasures.
  • Arankele Monastery: Visit the Arankele Monastery, a tranquil Buddhist retreat.
  • Ridi Viharaya: Explore the Ridi Viharaya, a Buddhist temple known for its caves.
  • Ethagala: Hike to Ethagala, a scenic hill with panoramic views of the region.
