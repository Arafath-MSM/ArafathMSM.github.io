Matara District

Tourist Places

  • Star Fort: Explore the historic Star Fort located in Matara town.
  • Old Dutch Trade Center (Nupe Market): Visit this historic Dutch building that now serves as a market.
  • Weherahena Temple: Marvel at the intricate murals and the towering Buddha statue inside the Weherahena Temple.
  • Polhena Beach: Relax on the beautiful Polhena Beach and enjoy snorkeling and swimming.
  • Crow Island: Explore Crow Island, a serene spot for bird watching and nature lovers.
  • Dondra Head Lighthouse: Visit the iconic Dondra Head Lighthouse at the southernmost point of Sri Lanka.
  • Matara Paravi Duwa Temple: Discover the scenic Matara Paravi Duwa Temple located on an island in the middle of a lake.
  • Matara Beach: Enjoy the sandy shores of Matara Beach and its beautiful sunsets.
  • Medawatta Beach: Relax on the tranquil Medawatta Beach away from the crowds.

Historical Sites

  • Star Fort: Learn about the history of the Star Fort, which dates back to the Dutch colonial era.
  • Old Dutch Trade Center (Nupe Market): Explore the historic Dutch architecture at Nupe Market.

Camping Spots

  • None: Camping options are limited in Matara District.

5-Star Hotels

  • None: Currently, there are no 5-star hotels in Matara District.

Explore the historical sites, beautiful beaches, and temples of Matara District, and enjoy the coastal charm of this region.
