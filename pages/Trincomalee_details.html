Trincomalee District

Tourist Places

  • Manayaweli Bay: A scenic bay with calm waters, ideal for swimming and relaxation.
  • Thirukoneswaram Kovil: An ancient Hindu temple with a stunning oceanfront location.
  • Kanniya Hot Springs: Natural hot springs known for their therapeutic properties.
  • Pigeon Island National Park: A marine national park for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts.
  • St. Mary's Cathedral: A beautiful Catholic cathedral with historical significance.
  • Trincomalee Beach: A pristine beach with clear waters and water sports activities.

Historical Places

  • Fort Frederick: An old fort built by the Portuguese in the 17th century.

Camping Spots

  • Pigeon Island National Park: Camp on the island for a unique overnight experience.

5-Star Hotels

  • Trinco Blu by Cinnamon: A luxurious beachfront hotel with modern amenities.
  • Anantamaa Hotel: A 5-star hotel offering splendid views and exceptional service.

Discover the beauty of Trincomalee District, known for its pristine beaches, rich cultural heritage, and excellent accommodation options.
