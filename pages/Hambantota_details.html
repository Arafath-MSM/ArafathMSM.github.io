Hambantota District

Tourist Places

  • Yala National Park: Explore one of the most famous national parks in Sri Lanka, known for its wildlife.
  • Ridiyagama Safari Park: Visit this safari park to see a variety of animals in a natural setting.
  • Mirijjawila Botanic Gardens: Enjoy a leisurely stroll in these beautiful botanical gardens.
  • Dry Zone Botanic Gardens - Hambantota: Discover unique plants and landscapes in this botanical garden.
  • Walawe River Safari: Take a river safari along the scenic Walawe River.
  • Agro Technology Park: Learn about modern agricultural practices at this technology park.

Historical Sites

  • None: Hambantota District is not primarily known for historical sites.

Camping Spots

  • None: Camping options are limited in Hambantota District.

5-Star Hotels

  • None: Currently, there are no 5-star hotels in Hambantota District.
