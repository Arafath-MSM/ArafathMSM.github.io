Ratnapura District

Tourist Places

  • Sinharaja Forest Reserve: Explore the lush rainforests of Sinharaja, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a biodiversity hotspot.
  • Udawalawe National Park: Go on a safari in Udawalawe National Park and spot elephants and diverse wildlife.
  • Boptah Ella Falls: Enjoy the stunning Boptah Ella Falls, surrounded by scenic beauty.
  • Adam’s Peak (Sri Pada): Hike to the sacred Adam’s Peak and witness the breathtaking sunrise.
  • Kitulgala: Experience adventure and water activities in the picturesque town of Kitulgala.
  • Maha Saman Devalaya: Visit the Maha Saman Devalaya, a historic Buddhist temple in Ratnapura.
  • Bambarakanda Waterfalls: Marvel at Bambarakanda Waterfalls, the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka.
  • Gem Mines: Explore the gem mines of Ratnapura, known as the "City of Gems."
  • Batadombalena: Visit the Batadombalena archaeological site and caves with ancient human remains.
  • National Museum Of Ratnapura: Discover the cultural and historical artifacts at the National Museum Of Ratnapura.

Historical Sites

  • Maha Saman Devalaya: Explore the historical Maha Saman Devalaya, a place of worship.
  • Batadombalena: Discover the archaeological significance of Batadombalena caves.

Camping Spots

  • None: Camping options are limited in Ratnapura District.
