Anuradhapura District

Tourist Places

  • Sri Maha Bodhi: Visit the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi, one of the oldest trees in the world.
  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress: Explore the magnificent Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Ruwanweliseya: Marvel at the grandeur of the Ruwanweliseya stupa, an iconic landmark.
  • Jetavanaramaya Monastery: Discover the historic Jetavanaramaya Monastery ruins.
  • Thuparamaya: Explore the ancient Thuparamaya, considered one of the earliest stupas in Sri Lanka.
  • Abhayagiri Vihara: Visit the Abhayagiri Vihara, an ancient Buddhist monastery complex.

Historical Sites

  • Sri Maha Bodhi: Sri Maha Bodhi is a historic and revered sacred tree.
  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress: Discover the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya.

  • Abhayagiri Vihara: Explore the ruins of Abhayagiri Vihara, once a prominent Buddhist monastery.

Camping Spots

  • Minneriya National Park: Enjoy camping and wildlife safaris in Minneriya National Park.
  • Kaudulla National Park: Camp in Kaudulla National Park, known for elephant gatherings.
