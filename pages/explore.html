The Central Highlands of Sri Lanka

The Central Highlands of Sri Lanka are a breathtaking mountainous region that covers approximately one-third of the island's land area. This region is known for its natural beauty, diverse landscapes, and unique attractions.

Key highlights of the Central Highlands include:

  • Sri Lanka's highest peaks, including Pidurutalagala at 2,524 meters.
  • The majestic Mahaweli River, Sri Lanka's longest river.
  • Lush tea plantations in regions like Nuwara Eliya.
  • Abundant wildlife and pristine forests.

Exploring the Central Highlands offers a range of activities, from hiking and trekking to wildlife safaris and cultural experiences. Whether you're an adventure seeker or a nature enthusiast, this region has something to offer for everyone.
