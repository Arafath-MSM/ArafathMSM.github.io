Polonnaruwa District

Tourist Places

  • Gal Vihara: Admire the stunning Gal Vihara with its rock-cut Buddha statues.
  • Polonnaruwa Vatadage: Explore the historic Polonnaruwa Vatadage, a circular relic house.
  • Lankathilaka Image House: Visit the Lankathilaka Image House, known for its exquisite architecture.
  • Wasgamuwa National Park: Discover the diverse wildlife at Wasgamuwa National Park.
  • Minneriya: Witness the famous Minneriya Elephant Gathering in Minneriya National Park.
  • Angammedilla: Explore the Angammedilla National Park and its natural beauty.
  • Wasgamuwa: Visit the scenic Wasgamuwa area for wildlife enthusiasts.

Historical Sites

  • Gal Vihara: Gal Vihara features impressive rock sculptures of Buddha.
  • Polonnaruwa Vatadage: Discover the ancient Polonnaruwa Vatadage, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Lankathilaka Image House: Explore the historic Lankathilaka Image House and its intricate carvings.

Camping Spots

  • Wasgamuwa National Park: Camp in Wasgamuwa National Park to experience the wilderness.
  • Angammedilla National Park: Enjoy camping in the beautiful Angammedilla National Park.

5-Star Hotels

  • Aliya Resort & Spa: Stay at the luxurious Aliya Resort & Spa for a comfortable experience.
  • Hotel Sudu Araliya: Find comfort and elegance at Hotel Sudu Araliya in Polonnaruwa.
  • Ruins Chaaya Hotel: Enjoy a relaxing stay at the Ruins Chaaya Hotel.
  • Kandalama Hotel: Experience luxury at the Kandalama Hotel, located nearby.

Discover the rich history and natural beauty of Polonnaruwa District with its ancient sites, national parks, and comfortable 5-star hotels.
