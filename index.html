Visit Central Side

The Central Highlands of Sri Lanka are a rugged and mountainous region that covers about one-third of the island's land area. The highest mountains in Sri Lanka are located in this region, including Pidurutalagala (2,524 meters), the country's highest peak. The Central Highlands are also home to many important rivers, including the Mahaweli Ganga, which is the longest river in Sri Lanka
